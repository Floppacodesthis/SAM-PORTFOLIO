TOYOTA SUPRA MK4

  • Engine: 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six
  • 0-60 mph Acceleration: Approximately 4.1 seconds
  • Horsepower: 382 hp
  • 8-speed automatic, 6-speed manual
  • Top Speed: Electronically limited to 155 mph

The Toyota Supra MK4 (A80), produced from 1993-2002, is a legendary sports car with a 3.0L I6 engine, offering both turbocharged (276 hp) and naturally aspirated (220 hp) variants.
Known for its strong performance, it accelerates 0-60 mph in 4.6 seconds and has immense tuning potential. The Supra MK4 gained fame for its role in *Fast & Furious* and remains a cult classic among car enthusiasts.

NISSAN SKYLINE R34

  • Engine: 2.6-liter twin-turbocharged inline-six (RB26DETT)
  • 0-60 mph Acceleration: Approximately 4.6 seconds
  • Power Output: 280 horsepower at 6,800 rpm
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual
  • Top Speed: 156 mph (251 km/h)

The **Nissan Skyline R34**, produced from 1999 to 2002, is a highly regarded sports car known for its advanced technology and performance.
It features a **2.6L RB26DETT** twin-turbocharged inline-6 engine, producing around **280 hp** and **392 Nm of torque**. With a 0-60 mph time of approximately **5.4 seconds**, it is celebrated for its handling, robust tuning potential, and iconic status in the **Fast & Furious** franchise. The R34 Skyline remains a symbol of Japanese performance and motorsport engineering.

KOENIGSEGG JESKO ABSOLUT

  • Engine: 5.0-liter twin-turbo V8
  • Acceleration (0-60 mph): Under 2.5 seconds
  • Power Output: 1,600 hp (1,177 kW) on E85 biofuel, 1,280 hp (953 kW) on regular gasoline.
  • Transmission: Light Speed Transmission (LST), 9-speed multi-clutch system
  • Top Speed: 300+ mph (483+ km/h) (Target)

The Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut is a hypercar designed for top-speed performance, introduced in 2020.
Powered by a 5.0L twin-turbo V8 engine, it produces 1,600 hp on E85 fuel and 1,280 hp on regular fuel. With a top speed estimated to exceed 300 mph (480 km/h), the Jesko Absolut is built for unmatched aerodynamics and handling.
Its lightweight carbon fiber structure, advanced suspension, and cutting-edge technology make it one of the most extreme performance cars ever created.

KOENIGSEGG AGERA

  • Engine: 5.0L twin-turbocharged V8
  • Acceleration (0-60 mph): 2.8 seconds
  • Power Output (Horsepower): 1,160 hp (varies slightly based on specific model)
  • Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch transmission
  • Top Speed: 273 mph (440 km/h)

KOENIGSEGG REGERA

  • Engine: 5.0L twin-turbocharged V8 + 3 electric motors (Hybrid system)
  • Acceleration (0-60 mph): 2.8 seconds
  • Power Output (Horsepower): 1,500 hp (combined total of gas engine and electric motors)
  • Transmission: Direct Drive (No traditional gearbox)
  • Top Speed: 255 mph (410 km/h)

BUGATTI VISON GRAN TURISMO

  • Acceleration (0-60 mph): Around 2.5 seconds
  • Power Output (horsepower): 1,500 hp
  • Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch automatic
  • Top Speed: Estimated at 267 mph (430 km/h)
